## Verification Methodology

1. **Systematic Selection:** Manufacturers processed in alphabetical batches of 15

3. **Multi-Source Cross-Reference:** Minimum 2-3 independent sources
4. **Official Source Priority:** Manufacturer websites given highest weight
5. **Documentation:** All findings recorded with source citations
